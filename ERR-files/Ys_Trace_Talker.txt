Weft Trace 05: The Trace Talker

Scene 1: 
Ys wakes up next to Bravo in his own soulspace. They talk, they wish they had longer, but they both have work to get to. 
Establish: 
This is Ys's soulspace, a reflection of his self
He can affect himself by changing it and his thoughts change it back
This is an unusual thing to do
Bravo and Ys are a pair, and they love each other
Ys is the organized, practical one
Bravo's job is social, difficult, and fun, and he's on the verge of a breakthrough

Scene 2:
Ys enters the trace that landed while he was resting. There's a request from a close friend to recover a shared key. Ys finds it, but also finds that this guy secretly hated his friend
Establish:
Ys's job and specialty is investigating the traces of dead users
Death in the Weft has one source these days, and that's errors
Errors leave a trace - the remnants of a soulspace - broken and corrupted
Ys is very good at what he does

Scene 3:
Ys meets with the close friend at a meeting-place in the Archives. He hands over the key, but can't restrain himself from saying what he learned.
Establish: 
Ys is not possessed of Bravo's social graces and tends to put his foot in his mouth.
The Archives have been turned fully to the war effort, resulting in cutbacks, stress, and surveillance. Paranoia is in the air, even more so than the rest of the Weft.
Y's work is in-demand and solitary; he talks to few people besides clients and Bravo.

Scene 4:
Ys returns to his soulspace. There's a new trace here, and it's Bravo's. Attached, is deep condolences, and an urgent request.
Establish:
Ys is emotionally stunted and immerses himself in his work
He drifted in the Weft for a while before Bravo found him
He was a master puzzlesmith - and Bravo a team solver - before the crisis
Parts of Bravo's soulspace can be brought into his, and he does. Normally he doesn't do that.

Scene 5:
Ys meets with Bravo's team. They try to console him, but can't. He tries to resume Bravo's work, but can't. He leaves quickly and promises to help.
Establish: 
C is another soulwalker. She helped teach Ys, and knows him better than most.
Bravo's work required a certain instinct that no one else could match
Ys is going through it and reacting in all the wrong ways

Scene 6:
Ys is in Bravo's soulspace again. He's put his normal queue on hold. He tries to learn from Bravo's memories, but gets lost in a memory of them together. He makes the first swap.
Establish:
Swaps can be made, and they immediately affect who Ys is
Swaps are bad for the fabric of the soulspace, and undoing them especially is fragile business
Bravo loved Ys so much
